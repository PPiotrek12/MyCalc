How to use calculator:
+

    Write your operation in empty field in the middle of the screen.
    Operation is splitted to a couple of moves, if it is more complicated, calculator will do more moves.
    You will see three columns of fields:
  1. Lp shows you numer of current move
  2. Result is showing you what is the reult of operation from “operation” field.
  3. Operation is projecting what operation it is doing in this move

How to use other functions:
+

  1. You have to fill an empty field on the left.
  2. Then score original and the final units.
  3. Results will be in the right field. Maximal numer of characters is 300.
